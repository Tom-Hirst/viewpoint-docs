**What is Viewpoint**

Today’s partners expect personalized experiences, but few vendors have the time of resources to write bespoke messaging for every partner. That’s where Viewpoint comes in.

Viewpoint has been built in the indirect channel by channel people who understand the blood, sweat and tears that go into crafting to-partner comms. Our unique matchmaking tech automatically distributes the right content to the right partner at the right time, optimizing partner engagement, with minimal effort.
